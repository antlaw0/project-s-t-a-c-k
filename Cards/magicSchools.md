# Magic Skill and Spell Cards
Initial draft for **Biomancy**, **Elementalism**, and **Necromancy**.

## Shared Rules Note
- **Summon cards are dual-purpose cards.**  
  When a summon spell is cast, place that card onto the battlefield in the chosen row. The card is both:
  1. the **spell card** that was cast, and  
  2. the **summoned ally** now in play.
- Summons are **Controlled Allies** and follow the normal **shared activation** rules.
- Buff spells that remain in play should usually be placed in the target’s **status row** until removed or expired.

# Elementalism

## Elementalism Skill Cards

### Novice Elementalism
- **Type:** Skill Card
- **Rank:** Novice
- **Equip Spell Slots:** 1
- **Passive — Elemental Attunement:** The first Elementalism damage spell you cast each round deals **+1 damage**.

### Apprentice Elementalism
- **Type:** Skill Card
- **Rank:** Apprentice
- **Equip Spell Slots:** 2
- **Passive — Shifting Elements:** Once per round, when you cast a **fire, ice, or lightning** spell, you may change its damage type to one of the other two.

### Adept Elementalism
- **Type:** Skill Card
- **Rank:** Adept
- **Equip Spell Slots:** 3
- **Passive — Reactive Veil:** The first time each round you cast an Elementalism spell, gain **+1 Defense** until the start of your next turn.

### Master Elementalism
- **Type:** Skill Card
- **Rank:** Master
- **Equip Spell Slots:** 3
- **Passive — Intensify:** Once per round, when you cast an Elementalism spell that targets a single enemy, deal **1 damage of the same type** to another enemy on the same row.

### Grandmaster Elementalism
- **Type:** Skill Card
- **Rank:** Grandmaster
- **Equip Spell Slots:** 4
- **Passive — Cataclysm:** The first Elementalism spell you cast each combat gains **+2 damage**, or grants **+2 Defense** instead if it is a defensive spell.
