## Combat Domains
1. **Biomancy**

   * Life manipulation
   * Healing, regeneration, adaptation, biological effects

2. **Necromancy**

   * Death manipulation
   * Undead, life drain, decay, curses

3. **Elementalism**

   * Fire, water, air, earth, and other elemental forces
   * Offensive, defensive, utility, battlefield control

4. **Illusion**

   * Perception manipulation
   * Concealment, deception, sensory distortion

5. **Transfiguration**

   * Alteration and transmutation
   * Matter and form manipulation

6. **Divination**

   * Knowledge, foresight, revelation, detection

## Elementalism

Novice spells:

Fire Bolt
Deal 1 fire damage to one target.
The bread-and-butter attack spell.
 
Ice Shard
Deal 1 damage to one target.
Simple and reliable. Doesn't have to be fire.
 
Spark
Deal 1 lightning damage to one target.
Useful later if some enemies are vulnerable or resistant to certain elements.
 
Arcane Missile
Deal 1 damage to one target.
A generic magical attack unaffected by elemental interactions.
 

Earthen Shield
Buff
+1 defense to one ally until that ally takes damage then remove



Bone Servant
Discard one defeated enemy from the dungeon discard pile to bypass one obstacle, trap, locked door, 

speak with the dead
After a dealing the death blow to an enemy, you may ask it to reveal any traps to you. The next trap card drawn from the dungeon deck is automatically avoided
Gold digger
After dealing the death blow to an enemy, interigate it to learn where treasure is in the dungeon, receive 1 currency. (scales with levels so adept level version grants 3 currency instead of 1 and so on)


Dark Deal
Return one defeated enemy from the dungeon discard pile back to the dungeon deck to bypass one obstacle, trap, locked door, or hazard.
This places one enemy card back in play in the deck to pay the price for the positive effect.

Corpse Shield
Before resolving a trap, discard one defeated enemy from the dungeon discard pile to cancel or reduce that trap.

Whispers from the grave
Look at the top card from the enemy discard pile and choose whether or not to place it at the bottom of the deck instead.
