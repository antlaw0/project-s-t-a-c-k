# Project S.T.A.C.K.

## Initial Marksman Discipline Skill and Ability Cards

## Discipline Identity

The **Marksman** is a ranged martial discipline built around:

* Precise opening attacks
* Positioning and row control
* Exploiting enemies affected by negative statuses
* Weapon-specific combat styles
* Finishing wounded or vulnerable targets

Marksman abilities use the equipped weapon’s printed damage unless the card says otherwise.

---

# Marksman Rules Reference

## Ranged Weapon Attack

A **ranged weapon attack** requires an equipped weapon with the **Ranged** tag.

When an ability tells you to make a ranged weapon attack:

1. Use the equipped weapon’s printed damage.
2. Use the equipped weapon’s printed damage type.
3. Apply any bonus damage, Defense reduction, or other effects listed on the ability.
4. Resolve Heat normally under the core combat rules.

## Weapon Requirements

An ability may require:

* The `Ranged` tag only
* The `Ranged` tag plus a specific subtype

Examples:

* A Longbow has the tags `Ranged` and `Longbow`.
* A Shortbow has the tags `Ranged` and `Shortbow`.
* A Crossbow has the tags `Ranged` and `Crossbow`.

A Crossbow does not qualify for a Longbow ability, even though both are ranged weapons.

## Negative Status Effects

For this initial Marksman set:

* `Rooted`, `Blinded`, and `Bleeding` do not stack with themselves.
* If an ability would apply one of those statuses to a target that already has it, the status is not applied again.
* Poison follows its own stackable Poison rules.

# Skill Cards

## Recruit Marksman

**Card Type:** Skill
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Recruit
**Ability Slots:** 2

**Equip Rule:**
This card may hold up to 2 Recruit Marksman ability cards.

### Trait: Opening Shot

The first ranged weapon attack you make during each shared activation gains **+1 damage** if its target has no Damage counters.

---

## Footsoldier Marksman

**Card Type:** Skill
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Footsoldier
**Ability Slots:** 2

**Equip Rule:**
This card may hold up to 2 Footsoldier or lower Marksman ability cards.

### Trait: Mobile Shooter

Once during each shared activation, immediately before or after resolving a Marksman Action that does not already move you, you may move 1 row.

This movement does not cost an action.

This trait does not bypass Rooted or any other effect that prevents movement.

---

## Veteran Marksman

**Card Type:** Skill
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Veteran
**Ability Slots:** 3

**Equip Rule:**
This card may hold up to 3 Veteran or lower Marksman ability cards.

### Trait: Controlled Target

Your ranged weapon attacks against an enemy with `Rooted`, `Stunned`, or `Blinded` gain **+1 damage**.

---

## Elite Marksman

**Card Type:** Skill
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Elite
**Ability Slots:** 3

**Equip Rule:**
This card may hold up to 3 Elite or lower Marksman ability cards.

### Trait: Clean Kill

Once during each shared activation, when one of your ranged weapon attacks defeats an enemy, reduce your Heat by 1.

---

## Champion Marksman

**Card Type:** Skill
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Champion
**Ability Slots:** 3

**Equip Rule:**
This card may hold up to 3 Champion or lower Marksman ability cards.

### Trait: Deadeye

Your ranged weapon attacks against an enemy with 3 or more Damage counters ignore 1 Defense.

---

# Recruit Marksman Ability Cards

## Steady Shot

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Recruit
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged

**Effect:**
Make a ranged weapon attack with **+1 damage**.

**Design Role:**
The basic reliable ranged damage option. It works with any ranged weapon subtype.

---

## Skirmisher's Shot

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Recruit
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged

**Effect:**
Move 1 row, then make a ranged weapon attack.

**Design Role:**
An early-game positioning tool for a Marksman before gaining the Footsoldier Mobile Shooter trait.

---

# Footsoldier Marksman Ability Cards

## Pinning Shot

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Footsoldier
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged

**Effect:**
Make a ranged weapon attack.

If this attack deals damage and the target does not already have `Rooted`, apply `Rooted`.

**Design Role:**
A control tool that helps the party contain enemies that need to reposition, close distance, retreat, or escape.

---

## Broadhead Arrow

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Footsoldier
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged + Shortbow or Longbow

**Effect:**
Make a ranged weapon attack.

If this attack deals damage and the target does not already have `Bleeding`, apply `Bleeding`.

**Design Role:**
A bow-specific pressure ability that trades direct bonus damage for a lasting negative status.

---

# Veteran Marksman Ability Cards

## Aimed Shot

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Veteran
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged

**Effect:**
Make a ranged weapon attack with **+2 damage**.

You cannot change rows during this shared activation.

**Design Role:**
A high-damage stationary shot. It gives up the Marksman’s mobility in exchange for focused damage.

---

## Piercing Bolt

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Veteran
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged + Crossbow

**Effect:**
Make a ranged weapon attack with **+1 damage**.

Ignore 1 Defense for this attack.

**Design Role:**
The core Crossbow identity card. Crossbows become the Marksman’s armor-piercing weapon family.

---

## Split Shot

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Veteran
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged + Shortbow

**Effect:**
Choose 2 different legal enemy targets.

Make a ranged weapon attack against each chosen target.

Each attack deals **-1 damage**, to a minimum of 1 damage.

**Design Role:**
A Shortbow pressure ability. It sacrifices focused damage to spread damage, Heat, and possible status-triggering attacks across multiple enemies.

---

# Elite Marksman Ability Cards

## Long Draw

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Elite
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged + Longbow

**Effect:**
Make a ranged weapon attack with **+3 damage**.

You cannot change rows during this shared activation.

**Design Role:**
The Longbow’s heavy artillery shot. It is powerful, direct, and deliberately stationary.

---

## Suppressing Fire

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Elite
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged

**Effect:**
Make a ranged weapon attack with **-1 damage**, to a minimum of 1 damage.

If this attack deals damage and the target does not already have `Blinded`, apply `Blinded`.

**Design Role:**
A damage-for-control ability. It is especially valuable against enemies whose attacks, abilities, or targeting become less dangerous while Blinded.

---

## Exploit Weakness

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Elite
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged

**Effect:**
Choose an enemy with one or more negative status cards.

Make a ranged weapon attack against that enemy.

This attack gains **+1 damage for each different negative status card** on that enemy, to a maximum of **+2 damage**.

**Design Role:**
A party-synergy payoff. The Marksman becomes especially dangerous when allies have already inflicted conditions such as Rooted, Blinded, Stunned, Poisoned, or Bleeding.

---

# Champion Marksman Ability Cards

## Rain of Arrows

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Champion
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged + Shortbow or Longbow

**Effect:**
Choose up to 3 different legal enemy targets.

Make a ranged weapon attack against each chosen target.

Each attack deals **-1 damage**, to a minimum of 1 damage.

**Design Role:**
The signature high-tier bow ability. It can eliminate weakened enemies, spread status effects, and create multiple Heat-generating attacks in one activation.

---

## Execution Bolt

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Champion
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged + Crossbow

**Effect:**
Choose an enemy with 3 or more Damage counters.

Make a ranged weapon attack against that enemy with **+3 damage**.

**Design Role:**
The Crossbow finishing move. It rewards the Marksman for targeting an enemy the party has already wounded.

---

## Deadeye's Patience

**Card Type:** Ability
**Discipline:** Marksman
**Rank:** Champion
**Activation:** Action
**Requires:** Ranged
**Card Behavior:** Positive Status

**Effect:**
Place this card in your Status Row.

The first ranged weapon attack you make this combat gains:

* **+3 damage**
* Ignore 1 Defense

After that attack resolves, return this card to the Skill Card it is equipped to.

At the end of combat, return this card to the Skill Card it is equipped to if it has not been used.

You cannot have more than 1 `Deadeye's Patience` card in your Status Row.

**Design Role:**
A preparation turn that creates a devastating later shot. The card’s location in the Status Row makes the pending bonus physically trackable without rotating, flipping, or marking the card.

# Marksman Progression Summary

| Rank        | Core Play Pattern                                   | Skill Trait       |
| ----------- | --------------------------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| Recruit     | Hit an untouched enemy first and establish pressure | Opening Shot      |
| Footsoldier | Reposition around the battlefield                   | Mobile Shooter    |
| Veteran     | Punish controlled and disabled enemies              | Controlled Target |
| Elite       | Reduce threat after securing a kill                 | Clean Kill        |
| Champion    | Finish heavily wounded enemies through Defense      | Deadeye           |
