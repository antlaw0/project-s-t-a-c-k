# Biomancy Skill and Spell Cards

## Biomancy Card Rules

### Casting Biomancy Spells

A Biomancy spell uses the caster's normal **shared activation**.

After resolving a spell, return it to its equipped spell slot beneath the appropriate Biomancy Skill Card unless the card instructs you to place it in a target's status row.

### Ally Terminology

For Biomancy cards:

* **Ally** means a Friendly Entity other than the caster.
* **Yourself or one ally** allows the caster to target themself.
* A card that says **one ally** cannot target the caster.

### Active Biomancy Buffs

Some Biomancy spells create a positive status effect.

* Place the spell card in the target's status row.
* The effect lasts until its printed expiration condition.
* When the effect ends, return the spell card to its equipped slot.
* A Friendly Entity cannot have more than one active copy of the same named Biomancy buff.

### Removing Negative Status Cards

When a Biomancy spell removes a status, remove the relevant negative status card from that Friendly Entity's status row.

Unless the card says otherwise, a Biomancy spell can remove the following negative statuses:

* Bleeding
* Burning
* Poison
* Weakened
* Stunned

# Biomancy Skill Cards

## Novice Biomancy

* **Type:** Skill Card
* **Rank:** Novice
* **Equip Spell Slots:** 1

### Passive: Gentle Remedy

Whenever one of your Biomancy spells removes a negative status card from an ally, restore **1 HP** to that ally.

---

## Apprentice Biomancy

* **Type:** Skill Card
* **Rank:** Apprentice
* **Equip Spell Slots:** 2

### Passive: Emergency Care

Whenever one of your Biomancy spells restores HP to an ally that is at or below half of its maximum HP, restore **+1 additional HP** to that ally.

---

## Adept Biomancy

* **Type:** Skill Card
* **Rank:** Adept
* **Equip Spell Slots:** 3

### Passive: Purifying Renewal

Whenever one of your Biomancy spells both restores HP to an ally and removes a negative status card from that same ally, restore **+1 additional HP** to that ally.

---

## Master Biomancy

* **Type:** Skill Card
* **Rank:** Master
* **Equip Spell Slots:** 3

### Passive: Deep Restoration

Whenever one of your Biomancy spells restores **3 or more HP** to a single ally, you may also remove one **Bleeding**, **Burning**, or **Poison** status card from that ally.

---

## Grandmaster Biomancy

* **Type:** Skill Card
* **Rank:** Grandmaster
* **Equip Spell Slots:** 4

### Passive: Living Surge

Whenever a Biomancy spell you cast restores HP to one or more allies, choose one different ally on the same row as one of the healed allies.

Restore **1 HP** to that chosen ally.

# Biomancy Design Summary

* **Novice:** Basic healing and direct treatment for common physical ailments.
* **Apprentice:** Stronger healing, basic defensive buffs, and combined healing-cleanse effects.
* **Adept:** Stun removal, group poison treatment, split healing, and temporary damage prevention.
* **Master:** Larger healing, multiple-status removal, row-wide healing, and ongoing regeneration.
* **Grandmaster:** Party-wide restoration, broad cleansing, high-value protection, and major distributed healing.
