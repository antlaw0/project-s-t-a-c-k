## Goblin Alchemist

**HP 6**

```text
1. Acid Flask
Deal 2 acid damage, ranged.

Target gains Corroded.

2. Dagger
Deal 1 physical damage, melee.
```

### Corroded Status

```text
Corroded

Reduce Defense by 1.

After preventing damage once,
discard this status.
```

That makes acid scary but not permanent for the whole dungeon.

---

## Goblin War Drummer

**HP 8**

```text
1. Coward's Rhythm
Lower the Heat of all Front Line characters by 1.

2. Warbeat
Raise the Heat of all Back Line characters by 1.

3. Drumstick
Deal 2 physical damage, melee.
```

This is much better than “increase aggression by 2.” It creates the goblin tactic:

> “Ignore the armored wall. Get the wizard.”

Perfect goblin goblinslop.

### Snared Status

```text
Snared
```

## Goblin Scrap Roller

**HP 7**

Construct, Goblin-made

```text
Attack: Rolling Blast
Deal 3 physical damage
to every character on the Front Line.

discard this enemy.
```

This replaces the Bomber. Now it is not a living suicide goblin. It is a squeaky death barrel with ambition.

# Tier 3 Goblins

## Goblin Assassin

**HP 7**

```text
1. Shadow Strike
If any character is in the Back Line,
deal 4 physical damage, melee,
to the Back Line character with highest Heat.

2. Blade Sweep
If no characters are in the Back Line,
deal 2 physical damage, melee,
to all Front Line characters.
```

Much cleaner than “attack normally.”

---

## Goblin Beastcaller

**HP 8**

```text
1. Beast Call
Summon Beast 3.

2. Spear Jab
Deal 2 physical damage, melee.
```

### Summon Beast 3

Draw 3 cards from the bottom of the dungeon deck. Put any **Beast** or **Goblin Beast** enemy cards drawn into play if there is room. Discard the rest.

For this to work, the goblin dungeon needs its own beast-flavored enemies, such as:

* Cave Rat
* Goblin Hound
* Mange Wolf
* Tunnel Bat

Do **not** use the Beast dungeon deck.

# Final Goblin Identity

The goblin dungeon should specialize in:

| Theme                          | Goblin Method                              |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| Back-line pressure             | Archer, Sneak, Assassin, War Drummer       |
| Heat manipulation              | War Drummer, Banner Thief                  |
| Cheap status effects           | Torchbearer, Hexer, Alchemist, Trap Tinker |
| Swarming                       | Captain, Warlord, Goblin King              |
| Disposable machines            | Scrap Roller                               |
| Summons without deck searching | Bottom-of-deck summon rule                 |

My strongest recommendation: **make Heat manipulation one of the goblin dungeon’s signature mechanics.** It gives them a distinct identity beyond “weak green enemies with pointy things.”
