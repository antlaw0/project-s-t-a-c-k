## Combat Domains
1. **Biomancy**

   * Life manipulation
   * Healing, regeneration, adaptation, biological effects

2. **Necromancy**

   * Death manipulation
   * Undead, life drain, decay, curses

3. **Elementalism**

   * Fire, water, air, earth, and other elemental forces
   * Offensive, defensive, utility, battlefield control

4. **Illusion**

   * Perception manipulation
   * Concealment, deception, sensory distortion

5. **Transfiguration**

   * Alteration and transmutation
   * Matter and form manipulation

6. **Divination**

   * Knowledge, foresight, revelation, detection

# ⚔️ Warrior Discipline

1. **Barbarian**

   * Rage
   * High offense with tradeoffs

2. **Knight**

   * Defense
   * Ally protection
   * Tanking and guarding

3. **Monk**

   * Unarmed combat
   * Mobility
   * Evasion
   * Body mastery

4. **Ninja**

   * Stealth combat
   * Distraction
   * Sneak attacks
   * Shuriken, staff, agile weapons

5. **Marksman**

   * Bows
   * Crossbows
   * Precision ranged combat

6. **Commander**

   * Leadership
   * Tactical support
   * Battlefield coordination

# 🕶️ Rogue Discipline

1. **Thief**

   * Stealing
   * Infiltration
   * Sleight of hand
   * Lock bypass

2. **Assassin**

   * High-risk, high-reward damage
   * Single-target elimination

3. **Saboteur**

   * Trap disruption
   * Debuffs
   * System sabotage

4. **Skirmisher**

   * Hit-and-run combat
   * Mobility
   * Positioning
   * Evasion
